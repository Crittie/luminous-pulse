## 6. Hashtag Strategy for X

X is not instagram. hashtag walls kill engagement. use 0-2 per post maximum. most high-performing posts use zero.

### when to use hashtags

| scenario | hashtag | why |
|----------|---------|-----|
| stat-response posts | #layoffs or #unemployment | joins the conversation sam is already reading |
| naming/permission posts | none | hashtags break the intimacy. let the words do the work |
| grounding practice threads | #mentalhealth | broad discoverability for the technique content |
| thread starts (post 1 only) | one relevant tag | helps discovery. don't tag subsequent posts in thread |

### recommended hashtags (use sparingly)

**primary (most relevant to our audience):**
- `#layoffs` — sam is searching this
- `#unemployment` — joins BLS / Kalshi conversation threads
- `#mentalhealth` — broad but relevant to grounding content
- `#jobsearch` — only on posts that directly reference the search experience

**secondary (use occasionally for reach):**
- `#careerchange` — broader audience, less shame-associated
- `#techlayoffs` — specific but high volume
- `#burnout` — adjacent audience
- `#grounding` — niche but highly relevant

**never use:**
- `#hustle` `#motivation` `#mindset` `#girlboss` `#entrepreneur` — wrong energy entirely
- `#hiring` `#resume` `#interview` — we're not a career account
- more than 2 hashtags on any post — this isn't instagram

### volume

- **original posts**: 5-6 per week
- **replies under viral posts**: 3-5 per day (this is where growth happens on X)
- **threads**: 1 per week (friday)
- **quote tweets**: 1-2 per week (stat-response posts work great as QTs of kalshi/BLS data)

### posting rules

1. **schedule everything for 8pm-12am ET** — sam's hours. daytime posts get lost.
2. **never post more than twice in one day** — we're not a content machine. quiet presence, not volume.
3. **never link in posts** — kills reach by 50-80%. the bio link is the only link. ever.
4. **replies are the growth engine** — the original posts build the profile. the replies under viral posts bring people TO the profile. spend more time replying than posting.
5. **engagement cadence**: post at 9-10pm → spend 30 minutes replying to trending labor market posts → let the algorithm work overnight when sam is scrolling.

| type | frequency | % of content |
|------|-----------|-------------|

## Quick Reference: The Stat-Response Formula

this is the core content strategy for X. use it every time a cold labor stat goes viral.

```
step 1: find the stat (Kalshi, BLS, layoffs.fyi, news outlets)
step 2: restate the stat (quote tweet or paraphrase)
step 3: name the feeling underneath it

the feeling is always one of:
- shame ("the silence between the question and your answer")
- invisible grief ("nobody sends flowers when you lose a job")
- identity fracture ("you don't just lose a paycheck")
- exhaustion from performing ("telling everyone you're fine")
- anger without a target ("you did everything right and it didn't matter")

step 4: end with a truth, not a CTA. no links. no product mentions.
         the truth IS the conversion. the profile click happens naturally.
```
