## Page 2: How to Use This Deck

### The Practice (2 minutes)

1. **Pick one card** each morning — or when you need it most
2. **Stand up** — posture matters
3. **Take one deep breath**
4. **Say the affirmation aloud**, three times, slowly
5. **Sit with the feeling** for 30 seconds
6. **Flip the card** and journal on the reflection prompt

The feeling follows the words. Not the other way around.

### Three Ways to Use This Deck

**The Daily Draw** — Pull one card each morning for 50 days. By the end, you'll have a practice that changes how you walk into every room.

**The Theme Week** — Pick one theme (Empowerment, Career, Morning, Anxiety Relief) and work through those cards for a week. Go deep before going wide.

**The Emergency Card** — Pin your top 3 to your desk, mirror, or laptop lid. Let them find you on the days you need them most.
