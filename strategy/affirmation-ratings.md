# Affirmation Ratings — Resonance & Shareability

> **Scale**: 1-10 (10 = instant save, screenshot-worthy)
> **Criteria**: Emotional punch, shareability, uniqueness, brevity, "say it aloud" power
> **Usage**: 9-10 → standalone quote cards. 7-8 → carousel slides, Reels, Stories. 6 and below → skip or rework.
> **Format**: Rating | Affirmation | Best Use
